Conditions I like to work in

I like a quiet work environment. I find it hard to do work that requires concentration if there is a lot of noise and distraction.

I like to be able to determine or bring my work equipment if possible.

I like to work in a fresh air environment, with plants and open windows.

I also like to work in a environment which contains natural elements like wood or a garden.

I also like a work environment filled with productive people and maintaining a productive vibe.

The times/hours I like to work

I am trying to start my workday at 8am. I usualy start my day at 6am and commute to work. While commuting I usualy try to keep up to date with the web. After I arrive I usualy work on side projects or algorithm challenges for about an hour.

I work best in the morning, but if I look after myself I can stay productive most of the day. Productivity will usualy decline after about 10-12 hours.

I don't mind being contacted at any time, but will likely only respond during week daytimes.

The best ways to communicate with me

I love direct communication. It usualy helps keeping misunderstandings and communication overhead to a minimum.

Else I prefer being contacted by email if it is work related. This helps me manage my tasks better. For private communication WhatsApp gets the job done.

In case of emergency do not hesitate to call me.

The ways I like to recieve feedback

I'd rather have difficult conversations than things be unspoken or inauthentic, so just be straight with me.

I like receiving feedback face-to-face

I see all feedback as a learning opportuniy so I like any feedback to include examples and also suggestions on how I or it could be done better or different.

Things I need

O² and H²O

Authenticity and people around me accepting their and others mistakes.

Things I struggle with

I find it extremely difficult to be able to work when having headache. If I find myself with headache I usualy try to sleep for half an hour or take some painkillers if the headache is not very dominant.

I sometimes struggle with remembering things, you could say I am scatterbrained. To combat this I usualy keep lots of lists, which help me remember everything.

If I don't understand the bigger picture or usecase behind what we are doing I find it hard to make smart decisions in the process.

Cache invalidating and naming things, also unnecessary process overhead. Minimum viable process anyone?

Things I love

I like hearing what others are working on and connecting up the dots of what we are working on in total.

I love pair programming and learning together as an experience.

I love working on silly little side projects. Testing new frameworks or features. I am really curious about all javascript.

If I find something I dont understand I will get to the bottom of it, to deepen my understanding of things.

Other things to know about me

While beeing introverted, I am an open person and you can talk to me about pretty much anything.

If problems arise in a project I will try very hard to keep pushing the objective instead of blaming others. Mistakes happen to everyone, its how we deal with it that makes or breaks a good team.

I like to resolve problems as soon as they appear, be it work related or personal.

I like to be friends with my coworkers if possible. This helps me communicate more directly. Also achieving this requires usualy more community activity than the usual work projects.
